Gravitational Attraction

This is a simple Gravitational Attraction implimentation in 2D.
Create attractors & repellers with LEFT & RIGHT clicks respectively.

Attractors (Green) exerts a graviatational force on a number of mover objects pulling objects into them while Repelles (Red) push objects away.
