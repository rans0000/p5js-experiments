Flocking (Steering behaviours)

Flocking Behaviours:
Use the sliders to tweak the influence of the behaviours.
Alignment: Nearby vehicles will try to head in the same direction.
Cohesion: Nearby vehicles will try to stick together until they are too close defined by the 'repel radius'.
Separation: Nearby vehicles will try not to collide with each other.
